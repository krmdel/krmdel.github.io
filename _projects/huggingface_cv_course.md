<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.6;">This open community course explores the fundamentals and latest advancements in computer vision, from image recognition and object detection to medical imaging and generative AI. Designed for learners of all levels, it combines theory with hands-on practice through collaborative, community-driven learning.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.6;">As a contributor, I helped to shape the fundamentals of imaging after my years of experience in computer vision and digital image processing to make complex computer vision concepts approachable, engaging, and applicable to real-world use cases.</p>
